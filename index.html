Frequently Asked Questions

I have already invested in mutual funds, equity, and FDs. Why should I invest in bonds?

Bonds offer stable returns and are less volatile than equities. They help diversify your portfolio.

 What are bonds?

Bonds are fixed income instruments representing loans made by investors to borrowers.

 How does the debt market work exactly?

Debt markets allow the issuance and trading of debt securities, providing funding and investment opportunities.

 What is the minimum amount required to invest in bonds?

Minimum investments vary, but some bonds start as low as ₹1,000 depending on the issuer.
